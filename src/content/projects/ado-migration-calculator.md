Migrating from Azure DevOps to GitHub is a decision that touches every engineering team in an organization. The business case is rarely just about licensing — it involves migration effort, retraining, workflow redesign, and the opportunity cost of delayed consolidation. This calculator provides a structured model to quantify those factors.

The tool walks users through inputs like team size, repository count, pipeline complexity, and current ADO licensing costs, then generates a total cost of ownership (TCO) comparison alongside projected ROI from consolidation. The model accounts for both direct costs (tooling, migration services) and indirect costs (productivity impact during transition, reduced context-switching after).

Built as a lightweight web application, the calculator is designed for solution architects and enterprise decision-makers who need concrete numbers to support a migration proposal — not a slide deck with abstract percentages.
